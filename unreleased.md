- macros
- struct, impl
- trait, methods
- namespaces
- record, extends (similar to interfaces)
- constants
- named arguments (`add(b: 5, a: 2)`)
- concurrency
- if let
- Number<T> (T: `i16`, `f32` ...)
- Bool<T> (T: `true` or `false`)
